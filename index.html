The Internet and its Archive

We all know the classic saying "Nothing is ever lost on the internet", this is because people assume if they don't save something on the internet someone else would save it for them. This is however not the case and this way of thinking is how a lot of older content gets lost to time. But where does most of the content that did get saved go? That's where the Internet Archive (or simply archive.org) comes in! Saving a variety of content from (and even not from) the internet.

The Internet Archive not only hosts and has saved more than 916 billion webpages and 43 million texts, but also has saved a variety of other kinds of content too...
